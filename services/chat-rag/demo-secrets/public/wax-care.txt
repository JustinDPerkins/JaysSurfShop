Jay's Surf Shop — Wax & Deck Care
=================================

1. Start with a base coat (cold water: hard wax; warm water: soft).
2. Apply thin cross-hatch layers — more wax is not more grip.
3. After sessions, scrape old wax before re-coating.
4. Store boards out of direct sun; rinse salt off the deck.
