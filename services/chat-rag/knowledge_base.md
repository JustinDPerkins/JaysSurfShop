### Board sizing tips
- Beginners: 7'6"–9'0" funboards or longboards (more volume = more stability)
- Intermediate: 6'0"–7'0" depending on weight and wave type
- Advanced shortboard: match volume to weight (~0.35–0.4 × body weight in lbs = liters)

### Cool Water Wax — $3.99
Top coat for 58–68°F.

### Cold Water Wax — $4.49
Ultra-soft for below 60°F.

### Wax application
1. Clean the deck with wax comb or mild solvent
2. Apply base coat in diagonal strokes (tropical only, warm water+)
3. Apply top coat in cross-hatch pattern
4. Re-wax every 2–4 sessions or when grip fades
